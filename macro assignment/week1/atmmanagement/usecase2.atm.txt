to check the balance
s1:insert the atm card
s2:select the language 
s3:select type of transaction and click on balance enquiry
s4:enter the pin
s5:select the type of account and click on saving account
s6:note the balance
s7:if the pin is invalid go to step9
s8:print and collect  the receipt if you want 
s9:remove the card 
s10:exit
